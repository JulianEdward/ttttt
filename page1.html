Ruth Binary

    Music has been my calling. I have taught music since high school. I knew what I wanted to be when I grew up - it was always something to do with music. Music is not only a god-given talent to me, but it is also an invaluable form of self expression. I enjoy seeing my students develop from zero to hero. They know nothing before and I love to see them change. Most of them were shy, but now they are more open and curious to explore music. I wanted to see them enjoy playing music and feel them. I wanted them to appreciate music and respect them.  Other than her very obvious cheerfulness, She also has the valuable characteristic of having a positive outlook in life. She takes challenges as they come and keeps a constant smile as she goes through them.

“Music is part of my life.” - Ruth Binary
